Jesus Christ and Catholicism

The Life of Our Lord Jesus Christ

The Nativity of Our Lord Jesus Christ

The day God came down.

The Word was made flesh.

The Prophecy of Simon

"And a Sword will pierce your very Soul." (Luke 2:35)

May we converse with Our Mother often - that we may learn to offer her our troubled hearts in exchange for the peace.

Miracles of Jesus

From Water into Wine at Cana to 5 bread and 2 fishes to walking on the water, Jesus performed a lot of miracles.

But the greatest miracle he ever performed is for You - in every moment of your life.

Declaration of Love

After He triumphantly entered into Jerusalem, Jesus tells his disciples how much he loves them.

May we also know in our hearts how much he loves us and to tread in holiness everyday.

The Institution of the Eucharist

Jesus institutes the Eucharist and the Holy Priesthood.

My Lord and My God.

The Agony in Gethsemane Garden

Because He knows pain; because He suffered for us, He can comfort us in our pain and sorrows.

Jesus wept (John 11:35): Our God is the God who knows how it feels to be hurt.

The Betrayal

Judas betrayed the Son of Man with a Kiss: Hail, Rabbi!

May we reflect on our sins - the times when we betrayed His Love by our sins.

The Crucifixion of Jesus

Calvary could not have been so far away on any day than that Friday.

You could not have been in his heart any other moment than when He was carrying the Cross.

Holy Saturday

"On Holy Saturday, the voice of God resounded in the realm of death. The unimaginable occurred; namely, love penetrated Hell.” - Robert Cardinal Sarah

May we have the trust in the Lord to wait for him, in all paths of our life.

The Resurrection of Our Lord

"And if Christ has not been raised, our preaching is in vain and so is your faith" (1 Corinthians 15:14)

May the Peace of the risen Christ be upon us always.

The Catholic Mission

"Go into the world and preach the Gospel to all the creation" (Mark 16:15)

When you kneel before an Altar, know before whom you kneel.
